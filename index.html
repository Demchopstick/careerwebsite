Video Game Designer

About

A Video Game Designer, or Game Designer, develops new video games for gaming systems, computer software, websites and mobile devices. Video Game Designers usually work for video game production studios using their creativity to come up with a cutting-edge game. They develop the overall concept as well as all the creative details of the gameplay, characters, scripts and plot
